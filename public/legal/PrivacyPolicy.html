Data Privacy Policy (Pre-Incorporation)

Data Controller

As the platform is currently unincorporated, the founding team acts as the individual Data Controllers.

Data Collection & Purpose

We collect profile and usage data to explore, iterate, and enhance platform functionality.

Third-Party Processing

We utilize AWS for secure hosting and infrastructure. We intend to use services like Apollo for profile enrichment and Google Analytics for analytics.

Consent & Compliance Transition

Under GDPR and Singapore PDPA, non-essential data enrichment requires explicit consent. Processing via Apollo or similar services will not commence until an explicit opt-in mechanism (compliance module) is deployed and accepted upon your next login after the module is put in place. We presently do not have an expected timeline for this transition.

User Rights

You maintain the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the founders via hello@thecarboneconomy.org.

Security

We take all reasonable efforts to secure the platform against unauthorised access. However, as the platform is pre-incorporation and still being built, the risk of vulnerabilities is high. We request that you do not provide anything on the platform which you would not be comfortable posting publicly on the internet.

Policy Updates

We will post modifications to our data practices directly to this policy; continued use of the platform after updates indicates acknowledgment of these changes.
